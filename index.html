About Us

Who We Are

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) Barangay Bangon, Pagsanghan, Samar is a youth-led council dedicated to empowering the young people of our community. As the official governing body for the youth, we strive to create programs, initiatives, and opportunities that promote leadership, community engagement, and positive change.

What We Do

  • Youth Leadership & Development – Training and workshops to hone leadership and advocacy skills.
  • Community Projects – Organizing outreach programs, clean-up drives, and civic engagement initiatives.
  • Sports & Recreation – Hosting tournaments and wellness activities to promote health and teamwork.
  • Education & Skills Training – Scholarships, livelihood programs, and digital literacy campaigns.
  • Environmental & Social Awareness – Climate action projects, youth forums, and awareness campaigns on relevant issues.

Our Mission

To represent, serve, and empower the youth by fostering active participation in governance, social development, and community-building activities. We aim to be the voice of young individuals in decision-making processes and provide platforms for growth, learning, and leadership.

Our Vision

A progressive and empowered youth community that actively contributes to local development, social responsibility, and nation-building.
